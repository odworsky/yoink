What Are Evolutionary Algorithms?

Evolutionary algorithms are used to develop AI for a variety of different scenarios. The idea is modeled off of the mechanisms of actual evolution in biology. Developers initialize a random population with a variety of relevant attributes, and determine which models have the greatest fitness.

Terminology

"A population is a set of candidates for the solution, and its representation is called genotype. A generation is a step in the process where evolutionary operations take place on the population."
